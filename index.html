Who We Are

Building Rwanda's Youth Leadership Ecosystem

Youth Advocates Network (YAN) is Rwanda's premier youth-led umbrella organization, uniting diverse youth organizations under a shared vision of empowerment and impact.

We serve as the bridge between young leaders and transformative opportunities, providing the platforms, resources, and networks needed to drive meaningful change across Rwanda.
